CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
NAME DESCRIPTION
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin,
    also known as Ibarra, was born and
    raised in the Philippines. However,
    he moved to Europe to study
    there for seven years.
María Clara de los Santos
y Alba

  • María Clara de los Santos y Alba
    is also known as Maria Clara. She
    is a young woman who studies in
    Colegio de Santa Catalina de Sena.
Damaso Verdolagas

  • Damaso Verdolagas is often
    referred to as Padre Damaso. He
    falsely accused Don Rafael Ibarra.
Don Rafael Ibarra

  • Don Rafael Ibarra is the father of
    Ibarra. He was falsely accused by
    Padre Damaso.
Don Santíago de los
Santos

  • Don Santíago de los Santos,
    otherwise known as Kapitan
    Tiago. He is a man who is
    religious, respected, and rich.
